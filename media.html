2018 — Russia

🇫🇷 France 4 - 2 Croatia 🇭🇷

The final was conducted on July 15, 2018, at the Luzhniki Stadium in Moscow. France was ahead 2-1 at halftime. In the second half, Pogba and Mbappé increased the lead to 4-1. Croatia managed to score a late goal, resulting in a final score of 4-2, but could not mount a comeback. Kylian Mbappé became only the second teenager, following Pelé, to score in a World Cup final. This victory marked France's second World Cup title.

Man of the Match: Kylian Mbappé | Golden Ball: Luka Modrić

2022 — Qatar

🇦🇷 Argentina 3 - 3 France 🇫🇷 (Won 4-2 on penalties)

The final took place on December 18, 2022, at the Lusail Stadium in Qatar. Argentina initially led 2-0; however, Mbappé scored twice in two minutes to equalize the match. Messi scored again during extra time, followed by Mbappé completing a hat-trick, resulting in a score of 3-3. Argentina triumphed in the penalty shootout 4-2, granting Messi his first World Cup title. This match is widely regarded as the greatest World Cup final ever contested.

Man of the Match: Lionel Messi | Golden Ball: Lionel Messi | Golden Boot: Kylian Mbappé (8 goals)
