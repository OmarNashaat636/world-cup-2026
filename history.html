The Journey

The FIFA World Cup was first held in 1930, after FIFA president Jules Rimet decided to stage an international football tournament. The inaugural competition took place in Uruguay, where the hosts also became the first-ever champions. Since then, the tournament has been held every four years, except in 1942 and 1946 due to World War II.
